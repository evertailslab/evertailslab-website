How It Works
Clinical judgment built
decision by decision

Every scenario mirrors the real ward environment — dynamic patient data, clinical deterioration, and consequences that follow from your decisions.

STEP 01
🏥

Receive Your Patient

Students are assigned a real clinical case with vitals, labs, SBAR handoff, orders, and active events — exactly as on the ward.

STEP 02
⚖️

Make Clinical Decisions

Across five categories — Prioritization, Medication, Delegation, Consultation, Documentation — students choose the best action under real clinical pressure.

STEP 03
🤖

Receive AI Feedback

Every decision generates immediate, evidence-based feedback: clinical impact, optimal action, rationale, and the specific 2026 guideline cited.

For Institutions
Built for educators,
not just students

WARDWISE gives nursing school deans, clinical educators, and training directors the tools to manage, measure, and improve clinical competency at scale.

  • Cohort management — Create cohorts, assign scenarios, set due dates, and monitor every student's progress in real time
  • Institutional alerts — Automatic flags when more than 30% of students show weakness in a clinical domain
  • Export-ready data — Competency reports formatted for Oracle Fusion, SAP SuccessFactors, Excel, or DOH compliance documentation
  • Zero installation — Fully browser-based. Works on any device, any network, any location
  • NCLEX-aligned — Scenarios mapped to the Clinical Judgment Measurement Model (CJMM) for direct NCLEX-RN preparation support
