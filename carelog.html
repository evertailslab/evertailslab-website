knows this pain.

  • Paper checklists that get lost, damaged, or filled incompletely
  • RLE hours tallied by hand at the end of each rotation
  • CHED audit risk — competency coverage hard to prove with evidence
  • Student portfolios assembled in a binder at graduation
  • No single defensible record of clinical learning
  • Faculty hours wasted on data entry instead of teaching
What CareLog does
  • CI grades once on a tablet — everything else is automatic

  • CHED audit-ready — competency evidence behind every claim
  • Living portfolio updated in real time, graduation-ready
  • Single source of truth for skills, hours, and competencies
  • Critical safety steps enforced — miss one, the check-off fails
